Caring for Your Gemstone Jewelry
🧼 Gentle Cleaning: Use warm water and mild soap. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage natural stones.
🌙 Energy Cleansing: Place your jewelry under moonlight overnight to recharge the stones' natural energy.
🏠 Proper Storage: Store pieces separately in soft pouches to prevent scratching.
💧 Water Wisdom: Remove jewelry before swimming, showering, or exercising to preserve the metals.
